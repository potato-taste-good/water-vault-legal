# Drople - Support

Thanks for using Drople! We're here to help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I reset my daily water goal?
Go to Settings → Personal Info to adjust your daily target.

### My data is not syncing to Apple Watch?
Make sure both devices are connected via Bluetooth and signed into the same Apple ID. Open the Watch app on your iPhone and verify Drople is installed.

### How do I edit or delete a past record?
Go to the Records tab, tap on any entry to edit or delete it.

### The reminder is not working?
Check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings → Drople → Notifications. Also make sure the reminder toggle is turned on within the app.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Drople → Cancel Subscription.

### How do I clear all data?
Go to Settings → Data → Clear All Data. This action cannot be undone.
